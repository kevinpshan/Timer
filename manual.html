User Manual v2.2

This tool is designed to assist Timers and Ah-Counters during Toastmasters meetings. It is optimized for iPad but works on all modern mobile devices.

1. Switching Modes

At the top of the main screen, use the Tabs to toggle between the Timer and the Ah-Counter. Settings and Roster data are shared between both modes.

2. Managing the Roster

  • Add Members: Enter a name in the "Name" field and tap Add. Names are saved permanently to your device.
  • Quick Search: Type in the search box to filter the list.
  • Selection: Tap a name in the list to populate the name field instantly.
  • Delete: Tap the red ✕ next to a name to remove them from your permanent roster.

3. Using the Timer

Timing Logic: The screen will change color automatically based on Toastmasters standards:
  • Green: Minimum time reached.
  • Yellow: Target time reached.
  • Red: Maximum time reached.

Tap Commit & Exit to save the time to the Meeting Report. The timer will automatically reset for the next speaker.

4. Using the Ah-Counter

Select a speaker and their current role. You will be taken to the Tally Screen.

  • Increment: Tap the large center of any word card to add +1.
  • Correction: Tap the gray − in the bottom-right corner to subtract -1.
  • Custom Words: Use the input at the top to add a specific crutch word for that meeting.
  • Permanent Delete: Tap the red ✕ in the bottom-left corner of a word card to remove that word from your permanent list.
  • Word of the Day: Highlighted in gold to help you track its usage specifically.

5. Reports & Sharing

Access the Meeting Report or Counter Report from the main menu to see all recorded data for the current session.

  • Copy: Copies the report to your clipboard. A confirmation will appear.
  • Share: Opens the system share sheet to send data to Notes, Email, or Slack.
  • Markdown Toggle: In Settings, enable this to export reports as structured Markdown tables—perfect for advanced users and professional formatting.

6. Backup & Maintenance

Go to Settings to Export or Import your entire Roster and Filler word list. This allows you to sync data between devices or keep a backup of your club's member list.

Clear Meeting Data: This button clears the current session's logs. Use this at the start of every new meeting.
