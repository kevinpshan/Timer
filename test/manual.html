User Manual

Welcome to the Southern Dutchess Toastmasters Meeting Tools suite (v2.8.7). This PWA (Progressive Web App) is designed to streamline the roles of Timer and Ah-Counter.

1. The Word of the Day (W.O.D.)

The W.O.D. feature now uses ChatGPT-4o-mini to provide intelligent, club-appropriate content.

  • Randomize: Tap "Random" to pick from a curated library of 1,000 sophisticated words.
  • Search: Type a specific word into the search bar and tap üîç to fetch a custom AI definition and usage sentence for that word.
  • Contextual AI: ChatGPT is instructed to provide "public speaking" flavored definitions and family-friendly examples.
  • Select: Tapping "Select" pins the word to the top-left of the Ah-Counter grid with a gold "WORD OF DAY" badge for easy tracking.

2. Timer Functionality

  • Presets: Quickly select 1-2m (Table Topics), 2-3m (Evaluations), or 5-7m (Speeches).
  • Custom: Enter a custom time limit for non-standard meeting segments.
  • Visual Cues: The background turns Green at the minimum time, Yellow at the target time, and Red at the maximum time.

3. Ah-Counter & Filler Words

  • Tracking: Tap any card to increase the count. Tap the small "‚àí" to decrease.
  • Persistence: If you stop a session and start a new one for the same speaker, the app will ask to merge the counts.
  • Customization: You can delete fillers you aren't tracking by tapping the "‚úï" on the card.

4. Setting up AI Usage (ChatGPT)

Why do I need a key? For privacy and cost reasons, the app does not store a central key. By providing your own key, your meeting data stays private and you get your own dedicated AI "Assistant."

How to get your OpenAI API Key:

Step 1: Go to platform.openai.com and sign up or log in.
Step 2: Navigate to the API Keys tab in the left sidebar.
Step 3: Click + Create new secret key. Name it "Toastmasters App" and copy the key (it starts with sk-...).
Step 4: In the Meeting Tools app, go to Settings (‚öô) and paste the key into the ChatGPT API Key field.

Note: OpenAI provides a small amount of free credit to new accounts. Even after that, gpt-4o-mini is extremely inexpensive‚Äî1,000 word searches will likely cost less than $0.05.

5. Data Management

  • Settings: You can toggle Markdown Export for clean tables in Slack, Email, or Discord.
  • Backup/Restore: Use the Export JSON button to save your speaker roster and settings to a file. You can import this file on a different device to sync your data.
  • Clear Meeting Data: Resets all current logs and the selected W.O.D. for the next meeting. This does not delete your speaker roster.
