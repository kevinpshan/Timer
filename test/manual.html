User Manual

Welcome to the Southern Dutchess Toastmasters Meeting Tools suite (v2.10.3). This PWA (Progressive Web App) streamlines the roles of Timer, Ah-Counter, and Grammarian.

1. The Word of the Day (W.O.D.)

The W.O.D. feature uses ChatGPT-4o-mini to provide intelligent, club-appropriate content.

  • Randomize: Tap "Random" to pick from a curated library of 1,000 sophisticated words.
  • Search: Type a specific word and tap üîç to fetch a custom AI definition and usage sentence.
  • Contextual AI: ChatGPT provides public-speaking flavored definitions with family-friendly examples.
  • Select: Tapping "Select" pins the word to the top of the Ah-Counter grid with a gold "WORD OF DAY" badge for easy tracking.

2. Timer Functionality

  • Presets: Quickly select Table Topics (1-2m), Evaluations (2-3m), Ice Breaker (4-6m), or Speeches (5-7m).
  • Custom: Enter custom Green / Yellow / Red thresholds for non-standard segments.
  • Visual Cues: The background turns Green at the minimum time, Yellow at the target, and Red at the maximum.

3. Ah-Counter & Filler Words

  • Default Fillers: AH, UH, UM, ER, SO, LIKE, YOU KNOW ‚Äî plus the active Word of the Day.
  • Tracking: Tap any card to increase the count. Tap the small "‚àí" to decrease.
  • Customization: Delete fillers you aren't tracking by tapping "‚úï" on the card. Counts merge if you save the same speaker twice.

4. Speech Recording & Transcription

The app can record a speaker's speech, transcribe it using OpenAI Whisper, and automatically highlight filler words ‚Äî no manual counting needed.

  • Enable Recording: On the Setup screen, toggle Record Speech on before tapping START. The toggle appears above the role grid.
  • Recording: The transcription screen shows when active. Tap "End Speech & Analyze" when the speaker finishes.
  • Highlights: Filler words appear in red. The Word of the Day appears in gold.
  • Merge: Tap "Merge to Counter" to send the auto-detected counts directly to your tally ‚Äî no re-entry needed.

5. Transcripts

Every merged transcription is saved for the duration of the meeting so you can share it with speakers.

  • Access: From the Ah-Counter tab on the main menu, tap Transcripts.
  • View: Tap any speaker's name to open their highlighted transcript with a filler summary.
  • Share: Tap Share to send via email, Messages, or any app. The format depends on your Markdown Export setting:
    • Markdown ON: Full markdown format with headers, blockquotes, and **bold** fillers ‚Äî ideal for Slack or email apps that render markdown.
    • Markdown OFF: Plain text with **double asterisks** around each filler word ‚Äî readable in any email or notes app.
  • Cleared with meeting data: Transcripts reset when you tap "Clear Meeting Data" in Settings.

6. Setting up AI (ChatGPT & Whisper)

Why do I need a key? For privacy and cost reasons, the app does not store a central key. Your own key keeps your meeting data private and gives you dedicated AI access.

How to get your OpenAI API Key:

Step 1: Go to platform.openai.com and sign up or log in.
Step 2: Navigate to the API Keys tab in the left sidebar.
Step 3: Click + Create new secret key. Name it "Toastmasters App" and copy the key (it starts with sk-...).
Step 4: In the Meeting Tools app, go to Settings (‚öô) and paste the key into the ChatGPT API Key field.

Note: The same key powers both the W.O.D. (GPT-4o-mini) and speech transcription (Whisper). Both are very inexpensive ‚Äî 1,000 word searches and dozens of transcriptions will cost less than $0.10.

7. Data Management

  • Markdown Export: Toggle in Settings for clean formatted output in Slack, email, or Discord.
  • Backup/Restore: Export JSON saves your speaker roster, filler list, club name, and API key. Import it on another device to sync. Transcripts are not included in backups ‚Äî they are meeting-specific.
  • Clear Meeting Data: Resets all logs, transcripts, and the selected W.O.D. for the next meeting. Does not delete your speaker roster.
