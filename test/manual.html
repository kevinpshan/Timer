Meeting Timer Guide

This high-visibility tool is designed for timing speeches, evaluations, and impromptu sessions. It scales seamlessly across smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

⚠️ Data Privacy & Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. To ensure your privacy, this app does not use a cloud database. Speaker names, club settings, and meeting logs are stored entirely within your device's web browser memory (Local Storage).

  • Device Specific: Roster data saved on a tablet will not appear on your phone.
  • Browser Specific: Switching from Safari to Chrome on the same device requires a new setup.
  • Clearing Cache: Clearing browser "Website Data" will delete your roster and logs.

1. Getting Started

Upon launching the app, use the Main Menu to navigate:

  • Set Your Club Name: Tap the blue subtitle to personalize the app. This name is used in your shareable reports.
  • Meeting Report: View the log of all speakers timed during the current session.
  • Clear Meeting Logs: Tap this at the start of a new meeting to wipe the previous session's data.

2. Setting Up a Speech

Enter/Search Speaker

  • Type a name in the input box; it will automatically convert to UPPERCASE.
  • The Roster: Saved members appear in a list. Tap a name to select it instantly.
  • Adding New Names: Type a name and tap Add. It saves to your device and stays in the box so you can start timing immediately.
  • Managing Roster: Tap the small ✕ next to a name to delete it from your local memory.

Select Speech Type

Choose from the presets (Table Topics, Evaluation, etc.) or use Custom Time to enter specific Green, Yellow, and Red minute thresholds.

3. Using the Timer

When you tap START TIMING, the clock begins automatically.

  • Color Transitions: The screen stays white until the Green minimum is met, turns Yellow at the midpoint, and Red at the maximum.
  • PAUSE/RESUME: Stops the clock for interruptions without losing the current time.
  • RESET: Zeroes the clock if the speaker needs to restart.
  • COMMIT & EXIT: You must tap this green button to save the time to the meeting report.

4. Sharing Reports

In the Meeting Report section, tap Share Report.

  • Mobile: Opens your device's native share menu (SMS, Email, Slack, etc.).
  • Desktop: Automatically copies the report to your clipboard for easy pasting into an email or document.

5. Advanced Tips

  • Large Screen Visibility: Place the device on a stand at the front of the room. The timer scales to be visible from the back row.
  • Offline Mode: The app works without an internet connection once loaded.
  • Install as App: Use "Add to Home Screen" in your browser settings to run the timer as a standalone, full-screen application.
